Supporting Java Service Wrapper Development (Please Read)

The Wrapper team has been working very hard to provide a well documented product which is stable, as well as easy to configure and use. We always make every effort to respond to all support and request queries in a timely manner and work with users to help resolve their problems quickly. All of this requires a surprising amount of time.

The Wrapper, being an open source application, is free and will always remain free. However, if you enjoy using the Wrapper or find that it is improving the value of a product or service that your organization provides, we urge you to consider donating to the Wrapper project.

In many cases the Java Service Wrapper is being used to support and improve the reliability of mission critical applications or being shipped with commercial products. If your usage of the Wrapper falls into this category we urge you to remember that your customers will most likely never even know that the Wrapper is a part of the product or service that they are purchasing from you, and can therefor not realistically be expected to provide any support back to this project. It would be appreciated if you could include a slight charge for the use of the Wrapper when billing your customers and then donate the money back to this project. Please contact us if there is anything you need to help make this possible either with your customers or within your own organization.

The preferred way to donate the Java Service Wrapper project is by using our Pay Pal account. However if you prefer another payment method please contact Leif directly via email.

We suggest a donation of $50 per machine, but you may donate any amount you feel to be appropriate by filling in the amount below.

By default we will respect your privacy. But if you elect to do so we will show our appreciation by listing your name and donation on our sponsor's page.
